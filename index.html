Welcome to the German Shepherd world

Did You Know??

  • They Were Originally Herding Dogs – Despite their popularity in police and military work, GSDs were initially bred for herding sheep.
  • Their Bite Force is Stronger Than a Wolf’s – A German Shepherd has a bite force of around 238-291 PSI, which is stronger than some wild wolves!
  • They Can Learn Commands After Just 5 Repetitions – GSDs are among the smartest dog breeds and can understand new commands faster than most other dogs.
  • They Have Webbed Feet – Their slightly webbed feet help them swim and gain traction on rough terrains.
  • They Change Colors as They Grow – Many GSD puppies are born with lighter coats that darken over time. Some even change patterns as they mature.
  • They Are Prone to ‘Lean on You’ – GSDs are known for leaning against their owners, which is a sign of trust and affection.
  • They Have a Double Coat – Their thick double coat helps them handle extreme weather conditions, but it also means they shed a LOT!
  • They Can be Trained for Almost Anything – GSDs excel in various fields, from search-and-rescue to therapy work, bomb detection, and even acting in movies.
  • They Are Super Loyal but Can Be Velcro Dogs – Once they bond with their owner, they prefer to stick close, often following their humans everywhere.
  • Their Ears Take Time to Stand Up – Not all GSD puppies have erect ears right away! It can take 5-6 months before their ears fully stand up, and some may never stand.
